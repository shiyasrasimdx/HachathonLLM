# Hi Grandma!

This is a cozy little helper for anyone looking for a new job. 

1. First, you show it the job you want and your own work story.
2. Then, you click a big "Start" button.
3. A friendly little window pops up to show you your "Score" out of 100!

It tells you what you did great and what little things you might want to add to your story to impress the boss. It can read all kinds of computer files, so you don't have to worry about that.

It's just like having a smart friend double-check your homework before you hand it in! It even uses a "Smart Brain" (AI) to make sure everything is perfect.
